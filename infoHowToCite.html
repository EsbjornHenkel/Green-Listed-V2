How to cite

If you use Green Listed in a publication, please cite the indicated article linked to the library that you used, as well as Panda et al., related to Green Listed:

Green Listed – A CRISPR Screen Tool.

Bioinformatics, 22 December 2016, doi: 10.1093/bioinformatics/btw739. PMID: 28414855
